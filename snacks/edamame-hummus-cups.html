Edamame Hummus Cups

⏱️ 5 min 📂 Snack

Crunchy, savory snack cups with protein and fiber

~200
Calories
10g
Protein
6g
Fiber
18g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup shelled edamame (thawed if frozen)
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • Cucumber slices or bell pepper strips
  • Optional: sprinkle of sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Spoon hummus into a small bowl.
  2. Top with edamame.
  3. Sprinkle sesame seeds if desired.
  4. Dip with cucumber or peppers.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Serve with whole grain crackers instead of raw veggies
  • Let them mash edamame into hummus
  • Mild hummus (plain or garlic)

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Pre-portion edamame into small containers
  • Keep hummus cups ready in fridge
