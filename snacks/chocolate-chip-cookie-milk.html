Chocolate Chip Cookie & Milk

~250

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🧒 Kid Friendly 🧊 No Cook 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⚡ Quick 🥨 Snacks ⚡ Under 15 min 🌿 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 bakery-style chocolate chip cookie (about 35g, under 180 kcal)
  • 1 cup 1% milk

Instructions

  1. Serve the cookie alongside a cold glass of milk.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Already perfect for kids — it's a cookie!
  • Use a fun straw for the milk
  • Warm the cookie in the microwave for 10 seconds for that fresh-baked feel

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Buy cookies in bulk and freeze
  • Thaw individual cookies as needed
