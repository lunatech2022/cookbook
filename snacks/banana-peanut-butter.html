Banana with Peanut Butter

🏷️ Tags

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 tbsp natural peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Slice banana.
  2. Serve with peanut butter for dipping.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • This is already kid-perfect
  • Let them do the slicing (with a butter knife) for independence
  • Use fun plates or bento boxes
  • Try almond butter or sunflower seed butter for school (nut-free)

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Not applicable — make fresh each time
  • Keep bananas and peanut butter stocked
