Chocolate Strawberry Greek Yogurt Bowl

~230

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup plain 0% Greek yogurt
  • ¾ tbsp cocoa powder
  • ¾ tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • ¾ tbsp mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, cocoa powder, and honey until smooth.
  2. Top with strawberries and chocolate chips.
  3. Serve immediately or chill for 10 minutes.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Let them stir in the cocoa — they love watching it turn chocolatey
  • Add extra chocolate chips as a reward for finishing dinner
  • Use fun-shaped bowls or cups
  • Call it "Chocolate Pudding Bowl" — the yogurt is the secret ingredient

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Mix yogurt + cocoa + honey in bulk, portion into 3-4 containers
  • Keeps 3 days in fridge
  • Add fresh strawberries and chocolate chips just before serving
