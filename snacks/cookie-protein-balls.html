Chocolate Chip Cookie Protein Balls

⏱️ 30 min 📂 Snacks 🍽️ 12 servings
~80
Calories
4g
Protein
1g
Fiber
12g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🧊 No Cook 🩺 Nutritionist Approved 🥨 Snacks ⏱️ 16-30 min 🌿 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup powdered peanut butter
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ¼ cup mini chocolate chips
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together. If mixture is crumbly, add 1-2 tbsp of water or almond milk to help with binding.
  2. Refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. Roll into 12 balls.
  4. Store in the fridge.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Let kids help roll the balls — messy fun
  • Use a small cookie scoop for uniform size
  • Call them "Cookie Dough Bites" or "Energy Balls"
  • Add sprinkles on the outside for extra fun

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Make a double batch — these disappear fast
  • Keep in fridge up to 5 days
  • Freeze up to 1 month — thaw in fridge overnight
  • Pack 2-3 in a container for school snacks
