Strawberry Banana Smoothie

⏱️ 5 min 📂 Shakes 🍽️ 1 serving
~280
Calories
18g
Protein
6g
Fiber
35g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
    • \n
  • 1 ripe banana
    • \n
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
    • \n
  • ½ cup milk or almond milk
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • \n
  • Optional: 1 tsp honey
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Add all ingredients to blender.
    2. \n
  2. 2. Blend on high until smooth (30-60 seconds).
    3. \n
  3. 3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
    4. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use vanilla yogurt for sweeter taste
    • \n
  • Add a few drops of natural food coloring
    • \n
  • Let them press the blender button (supervised)
    • \n
  • Serve with a fun straw
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Pre-portion frozen fruit into freezer bags
    • \n
  • Blend and freeze into popsicle molds
    • \n
  • Keeps in fridge for 24 hours
    • \n
