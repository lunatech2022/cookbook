Chicken & Veggie Wrap

⏱️ 15 min 📂 Lunch 🍽️ 1 serving
~450
Calories
35g
Protein
8g
Fiber
35g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 4 oz grilled chicken breast, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 whole wheat or high-fiber tortilla
    • \n
  • ½ avocado, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 cup spinach or mixed greens
    • \n
  • ½ cup shredded carrots or bell peppers
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp hummus or Greek yogurt spread
    • \n
  • Optional: sprinkle of hemp seeds
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Warm tortilla slightly.
    2. \n
  2. 2. Spread hummus or yogurt on center.
    3. \n
  3. 3. Layer greens, chicken, veggies, avocado.
    4. \n
  4. 4. Sprinkle hemp seeds if using.
    5. \n
  5. 5. Roll tightly, cut in half.
    6. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use a smaller tortilla
    • \n
  • Cut into pinwheels
    • \n
  • Let them choose which veggies go in
    • \n
  • Add cheese for familiarity
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Grill extra chicken at dinner, use for wraps next day
    • \n
  • Pre-chop veggies on Sunday
    • \n
