Chicken & Veggie Wrap

⏱️ 22 min 📂 Lunch 🍽️ 2 servings
~450
Calories
35g
Protein
8g
Fiber
35g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 8 oz boneless skinless chicken breast (2 small breasts or 1 large halved)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil
  • 2 whole wheat or high-fiber tortillas (8-inch)
  • ½ ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup baby spinach or mixed greens
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ½ cup sliced bell peppers (any color)
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced red onion (optional)
  • 2 tbsp hummus or Greek yogurt
  • Optional: 1 tsp hemp seeds or everything bagel seasoning

Instructions

  1. **Prep the chicken:** Pat chicken dry with paper towels. If one breast is thick, slice it horizontally into two thinner pieces so it cooks evenly.
  2. **Season:** Sprinkle both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Press seasoning into the meat.
  3. **Heat the pan:** Place a large skillet (cast iron or non-stick) over medium-high heat. Add oil and swirl to coat. The oil should shimmer when ready — about 1-2 minutes.
  4. **Sear:** Place chicken in the pan. Don't move it for 5-6 minutes. You want a golden-brown crust.
  5. **Flip:** Turn chicken over and cook 4-5 more minutes. Internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C) on a meat thermometer, or cut into the thickest part — juices should run clear with no pink.
  6. **Rest:** Transfer chicken to a cutting board. Let rest 3 minutes (keeps it juicy). Slice into thin strips against the grain.
  7. **Warm tortillas:** Heat each tortilla in a dry pan over medium heat for 20-30 seconds per side, or microwave 10 seconds wrapped in a damp paper towel. This prevents tearing.
  8. **Spread:** Spread 1 tbsp hummus or yogurt in the center of each tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border.
  9. **Layer:** Add spinach first, then chicken strips, carrots, bell peppers, avocado slices, and onion if using.
  10. **Season:** Sprinkle with hemp seeds or everything bagel seasoning.
  11. **Roll:** Fold the bottom edge up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll tightly from bottom to top. Cut in half diagonally.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use a smaller 6-inch tortilla
  • Cut the wrap into 1-inch pinwheels (easier to hold)
  • Let kids choose which veggies go in
  • Add shredded cheese for familiarity
  • Serve deconstructed: chicken strips + veggies + dip on the side

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Grill 4-6 chicken breasts at once. Cool completely, slice, and refrigerate up to 4 days
  • Pre-chop veggies on Sunday and store in containers
  • Assemble wraps fresh each day — pre-made wraps get soggy
  • Chicken can be frozen for up to 3 months
