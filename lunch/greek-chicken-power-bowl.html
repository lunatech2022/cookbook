Greek Chicken Power Bowl

Ingredients

  • 120g chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Pinch garlic powder
  • Pinch oregano
  • ¾ cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup diced cucumber, tomato, and red onion
  • 1 tbsp feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper for 15–30 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until browned and cooked through. Slice.
  3. While chicken cooks, whisk together the dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
  4. Place quinoa in a bowl. Top with vegetables and sliced chicken.
  5. Drizzle with dressing and sprinkle with feta and parsley.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Serve deconstructed: chicken strips, quinoa, and veggies in separate piles
  • Skip the red onion (too sharp for most kids)
  • Use mild cheddar instead of feta if they're cheese-picky
  • Let them assemble their own bowl — involvement = eating

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook 3-4 chicken breasts at once, slice and refrigerate up to 4 days
  • Pre-chop veggies and store in containers
  • Make dressing in bulk — keeps 5 days
  • Assemble fresh bowls all week in under 5 minutes
