Chicken Caesar Pasta Salad

📦 Batch Friendly 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🥗 Lunch 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⏱️ 16-30 min

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rotini pasta
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 120g chicken breast
  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • ½ cup cucumber, diced
  • 2 tbsp light Caesar dressing
  • 1 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh cracked pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden and cooked through, then slice.
  3. In a large bowl, combine pasta, romaine, cucumber, and chicken. Toss with Caesar dressing.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan and black pepper before serving.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use plain pasta (skip the dressing) and serve Caesar on the side for dipping
  • Cut chicken into small, bite-sized pieces
  • Skip the black pepper if they find it too spicy
  • Add cherry tomatoes for color and sweetness

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook pasta in bulk — toss with a tiny bit of olive oil so it doesn't stick, refrigerate up to 4 days
  • Cook 3-4 chicken breasts, slice and store
  • Pre-chop romaine and cucumber
  • Assemble fresh or pack as a cold lunch
