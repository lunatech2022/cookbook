Turkey Club Wrap with Honey Dijon Slaw

⏱️ 20 min 📂 Lunch 🍽️ 1 serving
~415

8g

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🌾 High Fiber 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🥗 Lunch 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⏱️ 16-30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 120g turkey breast or chicken breast (raw)
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp honey
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken/turkey until golden and cooked through.
  2. Combine all slaw ingredients and toss well.
  3. Warm tortilla slightly. Layer cooked chicken/turkey, lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and slaw.
  4. Roll tightly and toast in a dry skillet for 2 minutes per side.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Skip the Dijon mustard in slaw — use plain Greek yogurt instead
  • Cut wrap into pinwheels for easy holding
  • Serve slaw on the side if they don't like mixed textures
  • Let them choose their own veggies

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook 3-4 chicken/turkey breasts at once, slice and refrigerate
  • Make slaw in bulk — keeps 3 days
  • Assemble wraps fresh — takes 3 minutes
  • Wraps don't keep well assembled; store components separately
