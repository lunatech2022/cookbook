Berry Chia Oatmeal

⏱️ 10 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~380
Calories
14g
Protein
12g
Fiber
55g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats (old-fashioned, not instant)
  • 1 cup water, milk, or almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen — don't thaw frozen)
  • 1 tbsp almond butter or peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. **Combine base:** In a small saucepan, add oats, liquid, chia seeds, salt, and cinnamon. Stir to mix.
  2. **Bring to boil:** Place over medium-high heat. Cook 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until mixture starts bubbling around the edges.
  3. **Reduce heat:** Turn down to medium-low. Simmer 3-5 minutes, stirring every minute so it doesn't stick. Oatmeal is done when it's thick and creamy, and oats are tender (not crunchy). If too thick, add 2-3 tbsp more liquid.
  4. **Transfer:** Pour into a bowl immediately.
  5. **Top:** Add berries while oatmeal is hot (this slightly warms fresh berries or helps frozen ones soften). Add almond butter in small dollops. Drizzle honey if desired.
  6. **Stir:** Mix everything together so the nut butter melts into swirls.
