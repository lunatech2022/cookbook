Berry Chia Oatmeal

⏱️ 10 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~380
Calories
14g
Protein
12g
Fiber
55g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
    • \n
  • 1 cup water or milk of choice
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • \n
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
    • \n
  • Optional: drizzle of honey or maple syrup
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Combine oats, water/milk, and chia seeds in a small pot.
    2. \n
  2. 2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    3. \n
  3. 3. Transfer to a bowl.
    4. \n
  4. 4. Top with berries and almond butter.
    5. \n
  5. 5. Add sweetener if desired.
    6. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Let kids pick their own berries
    • \n
  • Add a sprinkle of cinnamon
    • \n
  • Use milk instead of water for creamier texture
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Make a big batch on Sunday, portion into jars
    • \n
  • Reheat with a splash of milk
    • \n
  • Keeps 4-5 days in fridge
    • \n
