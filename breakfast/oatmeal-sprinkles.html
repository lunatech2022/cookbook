Oatmeal with Superhero Sprinkles

⏱️ 10 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~350
Calories
12g
Protein
8g
Fiber
45g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
    • \n
  • 1 cup milk or water
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
    • \n
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
    • \n
  • Colorful sprinkles (the "superhero" part!)
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook oats with milk/water according to package (about 5 min).
    2. \n
  2. 2. Stir in chia seeds and flaxseed.
    3. \n
  3. 3. Transfer to a bowl.
    4. \n
  4. 4. Let kids add their own toppings: cocoa, coconut, sprinkles.
    5. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Call them "Superhero Sprinkles" — gives them power!
    • \n
  • Let kids measure and add their own toppings
    • \n
  • Use their favorite colors
    • \n
  • Add a drizzle of honey
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Pre-mix dry toppings in small jars
    • \n
  • Cook oats in bulk, reheat with splash of milk
    • \n
