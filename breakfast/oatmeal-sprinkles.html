Oatmeal with Superhero Sprinkles

⏱️ 10 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~360
Calories
12g
Protein
8g
Fiber
58g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (dairy, oat, or almond)
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  • 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp shredded coconut
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (walnuts, almonds)
  • Colorful sprinkles (the fun part!)

Instructions

  1. **Combine base:** In a small saucepan, add oats, milk, cinnamon, and salt. Stir to mix.
  2. **Bring to boil:** Place saucepan over **medium-high heat** (level 6-7 of 10). Cook **3-4 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until bubbles form around edges and break the surface. Watch for boil-overs — milk foams more than water. If it rises, lift pan off heat for 5 seconds and stir.
  3. **Simmer:** Reduce heat to **medium-low** (level 3 of 10). You want gentle bubbling — not a vigorous boil. Cook **3-5 minutes**, stirring every 30-45 seconds, until thick and creamy. Oats should be tender when tasted — soft all the way through, no crunchy center. If too thick (spoon stands up), stir in 2-3 tbsp more milk. If too thin (runny like soup), cook 1-2 more minutes.
  4. **Add nut butter:** Remove from heat. Stir in peanut butter and honey while oatmeal is hot — the residual heat melts the nut butter into swirls. Mix thoroughly so you don't get one big peanut butter blob.
  5. **Transfer:** Pour into a bowl.
  6. **Add sprinkles:** Let your child (or you!) sprinkle on toppings. The "superhero" part is making it colorful and fun.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Let kids pick their own sprinkles from a "topping bar"
  • Use their favorite color sprinkles
  • Add a face with banana slices and berries
  • Serve slightly cooled — test temperature before serving
  • Use cookie cutters to make shapes in cooled oatmeal

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook 4-5 servings, store in fridge up to 5 days
  • Reheat: Add 2-3 tbsp milk, microwave 60-90 seconds, stir well
  • Prep "sprinkle jars" — small containers with mixed toppings
  • Nut butter and honey can be pre-mixed in a jar
  • Don't add sprinkles until serving
