French Toast Sticks

⏱️ 15 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 2 servings
~350
Calories
14g
Protein
4g
Fiber
45g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread (thick-cut works best: brioche, Texas toast, or whole wheat)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup milk (dairy or oat milk)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp butter (for pan)
  • Optional for serving: maple syrup, fresh berries, powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. **Cut bread:** Stack bread slices. Cut each slice into 3-4 strips (like long french fries). Set aside.
  2. **Make batter:** In a shallow bowl (big enough to fit a bread strip), whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon until fully combined and slightly frothy.
  3. **Heat pan:** Place a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add butter and let it melt and foam, about 1 minute. Swirl to coat the pan. The butter should sizzle gently — if it smokes, turn heat down.
  4. **Dip bread:** Working with 3-4 sticks at a time, dip each stick in the batter for 2-3 seconds per side. Don't soak too long or bread gets soggy.
  5. **Cook first side:** Place dipped sticks in the pan with space between them. Cook 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Don't move them — let them form a crust.
  6. **Flip:** Turn each stick with tongs. Cook 2-3 more minutes until second side is golden and center is set (not wet).
  7. **Batch cook:** Transfer cooked sticks to a plate. Repeat with remaining sticks, adding more butter to pan if needed.
  8. **Serve:** Stack sticks on plates. Serve warm with maple syrup for dipping, berries on the side, or a dusting of powdered sugar.
