French Toast Sticks

⏱️ 15 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 2 servings
~320
Calories
14g
Protein
4g
Fiber
38g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole grain bread
    • \n
  • 2 eggs
    • \n
  • ¼ cup milk
    • \n
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • \n
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
    • \n
  • Butter or oil for cooking
    • \n
  • Greek yogurt for dipping
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Cut bread into strips (3-4 per slice).
    2. \n
  2. 2. Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla in a shallow bowl.
    3. \n
  3. 3. Dip bread strips into egg mixture, coating both sides.
    4. \n
  4. 4. Cook in a buttered pan over medium heat, 2-3 min per side until golden.
    5. \n
  5. 5. Serve warm with Greek yogurt for dipping.
    6. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Cut into fun shapes with cookie cutters
    • \n
  • Add a dusting of powdered sugar
    • \n
  • Serve with berries on the side
    • \n
  • Let them dip in yogurt themselves
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook extra and freeze
    • \n
  • Reheat in toaster oven
    • \n
  • Great for busy mornings
