Chocolate Orange "Hidden Power" Muffins

⏱️ 28 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 12 servings
~145
Calories
4g
Protein
4g
Fiber
20g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🌅 Breakfast 🌾 High Fiber 🧒 Kid Friendly ⏱️ 16-30 min 🌿 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 medium ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 large egg
  • ⅓ cup milk (dairy or oat)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • 1 tbsp orange juice
  • ¾ cup oat flour (or oats blended fine)
  • ½ cup barley flour (or whole wheat)
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp chicory root powder (optional — fiber boost)
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup zucchini, finely shredded + squeezed dry
  • Zest of 1 orange (about 1 tbsp)
  • 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds (extra seeds boost)

Instructions

  1. **Prep:** Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners or grease well.
  2. **Squeeze zucchini:** Shred zucchini on the fine side of a box grater. Wrap in a clean towel and squeeze out as much water as you can. You want it fairly dry — about ¼ cup packed after squeezing.
  3. **Mix wet:** In a large bowl, whisk mashed banana, egg, milk, oil, and orange juice until combined.
  4. **Add zucchini + zest:** Stir squeezed zucchini and orange zest into the wet mix.
  5. **Mix dry:** In a separate bowl, whisk together oat flour, barley flour, cocoa powder, chicory root powder, ground flaxseed, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  6. **Combine:** Pour dry into wet. Stir with a spatula until just combined — a few lumps are okay. Don't overmix or muffins get tough. Batter will be thick but spoonable.
  7. **Fill:** Divide evenly among 12 muffin cups (about ¾ full). Sprinkle with mini chocolate chips or hemp seeds if using.
  8. **Bake:** 16-18 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out mostly clean (a few moist crumbs are fine).
  9. **Cool:** Let cool in the pan 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or at room temperature.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Call them "chocolate cupcakes" — the orange makes them smell amazing
  • Let kids help zest the orange and mash the banana
  • Skip chicory root if they're not used to it (muffins still work great)
  • Add a pinch more cocoa if they love chocolate flavor
  • For toddlers, break into small pieces — these are moist but dense

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Keeps 3 days at room temperature in airtight container
  • Freeze up to 2 months — wrap individually in foil, then bag
  • Reheat: 20-30 seconds in microwave from frozen
  • Batter keeps in fridge 24 hours if you want to bake fresh in the morning
