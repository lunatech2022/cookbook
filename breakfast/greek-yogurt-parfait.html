Greek Yogurt Parfait

⏱️ 5 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~320
Calories
20g
Protein
12g
Fiber
28g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • Optional: 1 tbsp honey or granola

Instructions

  1. Spoon yogurt into a bowl or jar.
  2. Stir in chia seeds.
  3. Top with berries.
  4. Add honey or granola if desired.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use vanilla Greek yogurt for sweeter taste
  • Let kids layer their own in a clear cup
  • Add a few dark chocolate chips as treat

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Pre-portion yogurt + chia into containers
  • Add fresh berries in the morning
