Greek Yogurt Parfait

~320

📦 Batch Friendly 🌅 Breakfast 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🧊 No Cook ⚡ Quick ⚡ Under 15 min 🌿 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (2% or full-fat for creaminess)
  • ½ cup fresh or frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • ¼ cup granola (low-sugar preferred)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds or ground flaxseed
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. **Prep berries:** If using strawberries, remove stems and slice into ¼-inch pieces. If using frozen berries, let them sit at room temperature 5 minutes or microwave 20 seconds to partially thaw.
  2. **Sweeten yogurt:** In a small bowl, stir Greek yogurt with honey and vanilla extract until smooth. Taste — add more honey if you prefer sweeter.
  3. **Layer the parfait:** In a glass or bowl, add half the yogurt. Top with half the berries and half the granola. Repeat with remaining yogurt, berries, and granola.
  4. **Finish:** Sprinkle chia seeds on top. Serve immediately — granola gets soggy if it sits too long.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use vanilla Greek yogurt (skip the honey and vanilla extract)
  • Let kids build their own layers — they eat what they make
  • Use their favorite berries or diced peaches
  • Add mini chocolate chips as a treat
  • Serve in a clear cup so they see the layers

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Pre-portion yogurt into 5 small containers (1 cup each)
  • Wash and slice berries, store in containers
  • Keep granola separate in a bag or container
  • Assemble fresh each morning — takes 2 minutes
  • Pre-mix honey + vanilla in a small jar for the week
