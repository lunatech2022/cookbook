Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Overnight Oats

⏱️ 5 min 📂 Breakfast 🍽️ 1 serving
~445
Calories
39g
Protein
10g
Fiber
52g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🌅 Breakfast 🌾 High Fiber 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🧊 No Cook 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⚡ Quick ⚡ Under 15 min 🌿 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ¾ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • ½ scoop vanilla protein powder
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Zest of ½ lemon
  • Pinch cinnamon
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ¾ tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ cup blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a container, whisk together almond milk, Greek yogurt, protein powder, vanilla, lemon zest, and cinnamon.
  2. Stir in oats and chia seeds.
  3. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Refrigerate overnight. Top with extra berries before serving if desired.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use less lemon zest (¼ lemon) for milder flavor
  • Let kids add their own toppings — sprinkles, extra berries, or a drizzle of honey
  • Use a clear jar so they can see the layers — visual appeal increases willingness
  • Call it "Blueberry Cheesecake Pudding" instead of "oats"

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Make 3-4 jars at once for the whole week's breakfasts
  • Keeps in fridge up to 4 days
  • Do not freeze — texture gets mushy
  • Prep jars on Sunday, grab-and-go all week
