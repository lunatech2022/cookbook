Southwest Turkey Breakfast Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 100g egg whites (about 3 large egg whites)
  • ¼ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • Pinch garlic powder
  • Handful spinach
  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 75g turkey breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • ¼ avocado, sliced

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the eggs, egg whites, cumin, paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Lightly spray a skillet with oil over medium heat. Sauté spinach for 1 minute, then add the egg mixture and cook until set.
  3. Warm the tortilla. Fill with turkey, the egg mixture, salsa, and avocado.
  4. Roll tightly and toast in a dry skillet for 1–2 minutes per side.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Skip the cumin/paprika — kids often find spices too strong
  • Cut the wrap into pinwheels (slice into rounds) — easier to hold and looks fun
  • Let them dip in ketchup or mild salsa instead of spicy
  • Use a smaller tortilla for little hands

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook egg mixture in bulk, refrigerate up to 3 days
  • Assemble wraps fresh each morning — takes 2 minutes
  • Wraps do not freeze well (avocado turns brown, tortilla gets soggy)
