Salmon Quinoa Bowl

⏱️ 30 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 2 servings
~520
Calories
38g
Protein
8g
Fiber
35g
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (4-5 oz each)
    • \n
  • 1 cup quinoa (dry)
    • \n
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • \n
  • Lemon juice, salt, pepper
    • \n
  • Optional: tahini drizzle
    • \n

Instructions

  1. 1. Rinse quinoa, combine with 2 cups water in a pot. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, cover 15 min.
    2. \n
  2. 2. Toss broccoli with olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast at 400°F for 15-20 min.
    3. \n
  3. 3. Season salmon with salt, pepper, lemon. Pan-sear 4-5 min per side.
    4. \n
  4. 4. Assemble: quinoa base, broccoli, salmon on top. Drizzle with lemon and optional tahini.
    5. \n

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Flake salmon into quinoa so it mixes in
    • \n
  • Serve broccoli with a dip (hummus or ranch)
    • \n
  • Add a side of fruit
    • \n

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Cook extra quinoa — use for lunches
    • \n
  • Salmon is best fresh, but can be reheated gently
    • \n
