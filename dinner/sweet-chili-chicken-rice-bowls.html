Sweet Chili Chicken Rice Bowls

⏱️ 30 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 3 servings
~550

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🍽️ Dinner 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⏱️ 16-30 min

Ingredients

  • 1¼ cups jasmine rice (uncooked)
  • ¼ cup sweet chili sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 15 oz chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions. Meanwhile, whisk together the sauce ingredients.
  2. Heat ½ tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through. Remove to a plate if needed.
  3. Add the remaining ½ tbsp olive oil and stir-fry vegetables for 4–5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Return chicken to the skillet. Pour in the sauce and cook for 1–2 minutes, stirring, until everything is coated and the sauce is glossy and slightly thickened.
  5. Serve over rice and garnish with sliced green onions.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use less sweet chili sauce (it can be spicy for some kids) — mix with a little ketchup
  • Serve components separately: plain chicken, plain rice, plain veggies
  • Let them drizzle their own sauce — control increases willingness
  • Skip the ginger if they find it too strong

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Sauce keeps in fridge up to 5 days
  • Cooked chicken and veggies keep 3-4 days
  • Rice freezes well — portion into bags, freeze up to 1 month
  • Reheat everything in a skillet with a splash of water
