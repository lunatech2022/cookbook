Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta

⏱️ 30 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 3 servings
~590
Calories
46g
Protein
4g
Fiber
51g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🍽️ Dinner 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🩺 Nutritionist Approved ⏱️ 16-30 min

Ingredients

  • 6 oz dry penne or rotini pasta
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 15 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 tsp olive oil, divided
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ⅓ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • ¾ cup light cooking cream
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups baby spinach

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions; reserve ¼ cup pasta water.
  2. Season chicken with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden and cooked through; remove and slice.
  4. Add the remaining 1 tsp olive oil to the skillet. Cook garlic and sun-dried tomatoes for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in cream and Parmesan. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  7. Return chicken to the skillet and toss with cooked pasta. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed.
  8. Serve immediately.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Chop sun-dried tomatoes very small — or blend them into the sauce so they disappear
  • Use half the spinach at first, gradually increase
  • Call it "Pink Pasta" — the cream + tomato makes a pretty color
  • Serve with extra Parmesan on the side for sprinkling

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Sauce (without spinach) freezes well up to 1 month
  • Add fresh spinach when reheating
  • Pasta is best cooked fresh — doesn't freeze well
  • Cook chicken in bulk, slice and refrigerate up to 4 days
