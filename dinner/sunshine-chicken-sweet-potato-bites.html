Sunshine Chicken & Sweet Potato Bites

⏱️ 32 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 4 servings
~420
Calories
32g
Protein
8g
Fiber
38g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🍽️ Dinner 🌾 High Fiber 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🍳 30+ min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp orange juice (fresh)
  • 1 tsp orange zest (about ½ orange)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ½ tsp fresh rosemary, chopped (or ¼ tsp dried)
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ cup bell pepper, diced small (any color)
  • ¼ cup pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed (sprinkle on sweet potato)
  • Feta crumbles (if not dairy-free)

Instructions

  1. **Preheat:** Oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. **Cut sweet potatoes:** Slice into ½-inch thick rounds (like coins). You want them thick enough to hold toppings but thin enough to cook through.
  3. **Season sweet potatoes:** Toss rounds with 1 tbsp olive oil and ¼ tsp salt. Spread in a single layer on one half of the baking sheet.
  4. **Season chicken:** In a bowl, toss chicken cubes with 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. **Zest + juice:** Zest half an orange, then juice it. You need 2 tbsp juice and 1 tsp zest.
  6. **Mix:** In a small bowl, whisk orange juice, orange zest, olive oil, honey, rosemary, and garlic powder.
  7. **Coat chicken:** Pour half the glaze over the chicken cubes and toss to coat. Reserve the other half for drizzling after cooking.
  8. **Arrange:** Place glazed chicken cubes on the empty half of the baking sheet, spreading them out so they don't crowd.
  9. **Bake:** 18-22 minutes, flipping sweet potato rounds and stirring chicken halfway through (at 10 minutes). Chicken is done when internal temp hits 165°F (74°C) and edges are slightly caramelized. Sweet potatoes should be tender when pierced with a fork.
  10. **Build bites:** Place sweet potato rounds on plates. Top each with 2-3 chicken cubes.
  11. **Add color:** Scatter diced bell pepper, pumpkin seeds, and parsley over the top.
  12. **Drizzle:** Spoon the reserved sunshine glaze over everything.
  13. **Boost:** Sprinkle ground flaxseed or feta if using.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Let them build their own "sweet potato pizzas" — they pick the toppings
  • Cut chicken smaller (½-inch) for toddlers — cooks faster, easier to chew
  • Serve deconstructed: sweet potato rounds, chicken cubes, toppings in separate piles
  • Skip rosemary if they find it too "herby" — the orange glaze is enough flavor
  • Add a tiny drizzle of ketchup on the side for dipping (sometimes you gotta compromise)

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Sweet potatoes can be sliced and stored in water in the fridge up to 24 hours (pat dry before oiling)
  • Glaze keeps 3 days in the fridge
  • Cooked chicken and sweet potatoes keep 3 days in airtight containers
  • Reheat: 350°F oven 8-10 minutes or microwave 60 seconds
  • NOT ideal for freezing — sweet potatoes get mushy
