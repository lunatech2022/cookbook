Lentil & Spinach Curry

⏱️ 35 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 4 servings
~420
Calories
18g
Protein
15g
Fiber
50g (complex)
Carbs

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils (or 2 cans, drained)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can coconut milk (light or full-fat)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Serve with: brown rice or quinoa

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil until soft, 3-4 min.
  2. Add curry powder and turmeric, stir 30 seconds.
  3. Add lentils, tomatoes, and coconut milk. Simmer 20 min.
  4. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  5. Serve over brown rice or quinoa.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Serve over rice they like
  • Mild curry powder (or less)
  • Blend sauce smooth if texture-sensitive
  • Add a dollop of yogurt on top

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Makes great leftovers — flavor improves next day
  • Freeze portions for up to 3 months
