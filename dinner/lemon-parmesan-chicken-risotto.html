Lemon Parmesan Chicken Risotto

⏱️ 40 min 📂 Dinner 🍽️ 3 servings
~590
Calories
48g
Protein
4g
Fiber
67g
Carbs

🏷️ Tags

📦 Batch Friendly 🍽️ Dinner 🥩 High Protein 🧒 Kid Friendly 🩺 Nutritionist Approved 🍳 30+ min

Ingredients

  • 15 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 small shallot or ½ onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth, warmed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden and cooked through. Remove, rest, and slice.
  2. In the same skillet, heat the risotto portion of the olive oil over medium heat. Cook shallot and garlic for 2 minutes until softened.
  3. Add arborio rice and cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
  4. Add warm chicken broth, ½–¾ cup at a time, stirring occasionally and allowing each addition to absorb before adding more. Continue until the rice is creamy and tender, about 18–20 minutes.
  5. Stir in peas, Parmesan, lemon zest, and lemon juice until heated through and creamy.
  6. Divide risotto among bowls, top with sliced chicken, garnish with parsley, and serve.

🧒 Kid Adaptation

  • Use less lemon zest/juice — start with half
  • Serve chicken on the side, not mixed in
  • Peas are usually kid-friendly; if not, substitute with corn
  • Call it "Cheesy Rice with Chicken" — familiar language wins

📦 Batch Cooking

  • Risotto is best fresh — texture changes when reheated
  • However, you can prep: cook chicken in bulk, chop shallot/garlic, warm broth
  • Reheat leftover risotto with a splash of broth or water to restore creaminess
  • Keeps in fridge up to 3 days
